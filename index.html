Hello! My name is Sijan Thapa, and I'm a recent 12th-grade graduate currently studying full-stack development. I’m excited to introduce my first project, Text To Your Handwriting. This tool was created to simplify the assignment process for students, as I understand how overwhelming it can be to manage hundreds of pages of assignments. Often, teachers can’t check every assignment thoroughly, so I developed this free tool to make assignments more engaging and manageable. With a user-friendly interface and customization options, this tool allows students to personalize their text to match different handwriting styles. I hope this project helps ease the burden of assignments and offers a valuable resource for students.
